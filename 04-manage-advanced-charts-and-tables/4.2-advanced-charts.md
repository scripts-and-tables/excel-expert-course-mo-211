# 4.2 Create and Modify Advanced Charts

The MO-211 exam requires you to create specialized charts and perform specific technical modifications (such as adjusting bins or defining totals) within seconds.

-----

## 1\. Statistical Charts (Histogram & Box and Whisker)

### Histogram

  * **Create:** Select data \> **Insert** \> **Insert Statistic Chart** \> **Histogram**.
  * **Modify (Bins):** Right-click the Horizontal (Category) Axis \> **Format Axis**. Under **Axis Options**, set the **Bin Width** (the range per bar) or the **Number of Bins** as specified in the task.

### Box and Whisker

  * **Create:** Select data \> **Insert** \> **Insert Statistic Chart** \> **Box and Whisker**.
  * **Modify (Quartiles):** Right-click the series \> **Format Data Series**. Under **Series Options**, choose between **Inclusive Median** or **Exclusive Median**.

-----

## 2\. Process & Financial Charts (Waterfall & Funnel)

### Waterfall

  * **Create:** Select data \> **Insert** \> **Waterfall**.
  * **Modify (Set as Total):** To make the final bar represent a "Result" rather than an "Increase/Decrease," click the specific bar **twice** (until only that bar is selected). Right-click and select **Set as Total**.

### Funnel

  * **Create:** **Sort source data** from largest to smallest. Select data \> **Insert** \> **Funnel**.
  * **Modify:** If the chart looks inverted or disjointed, the error is in the **source data sorting**.

-----

## 3\. Hierarchy & Multi-Scale Charts (Sunburst & Combo)

### Sunburst

  * **Create:** Select data (must have at least two levels of categories/hierarchy) \> **Insert** \> **Hierarchy Chart** \> **Sunburst**.
  * **Modify:** Use **Chart Elements (+)** to toggle **Data Labels** or the **Legend** to ensure clarity in the nested rings.

### Combo (Dual-Axis)

  * **Create:** Select data \> **Insert** \> **Recommended Charts** \> **All Charts** \> **Combo**.
  * **Modify:** In the dialog, assign each series a **Chart Type** and check the **Secondary Axis** box for the series with a different scale (e.g., percentages).

-----

## Technical Modification Checklist (Exam Speed)

| Target Task | Menu Path |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Change Look/Feel** | **Chart Design** tab \> **Chart Styles** (Styles 1-12) |
| **Change Layout** | **Chart Design** tab \> **Quick Layout** (Layouts 1-10) |
| **Change Colors** | **Chart Design** tab \> **Change Colors** (Monochromatic/Colorful) |
| **Add/Edit Title** | Click Title box OR **Chart Elements (+)** \> **Chart Title** |
