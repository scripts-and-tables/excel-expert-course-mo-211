About the author

I've spent years working with Excel as my daily driver — building financial models, wrangling data, and automating the tedious bits with formulas and macros. While prepping for the MO-211 exam I found that solid, end-to-end material was either scattered across blog posts or locked behind paid courses.

So I wrote the course I wished I'd had: structured around the official Skills Measured outline, full of realistic scenarios, and free for anyone who wants to level up.

If a single lesson here saves you a few hours of confusion, the project has done its job.

Prerequisites

  • Microsoft Excel — Microsoft 365 version recommended (for XLOOKUP, LET, dynamic arrays).
  • Comfort with basic formulas and the Excel ribbon.
  • A willingness to actually open the workbooks and try things — that's the whole point.
